CREATIVE COMMONS GLOBAL SUMMIT 2015

A joyful celebration believing in the value of openness and sharing, creating a positive change with people from over 80 countries is taking place in October, in Korea.

2015.10.15(THU) ~ 16(FRI)

@ National Museum of Korea, Art Center Nabi and more

Main Program

  • Lecture

    Listen to the speakers from various countries abouthe messages of sharing and opening.

  • CC Exhibition

    Appreciate various creation applying CC license of artinsts, organized from Art center Nabi.

  • Forum

    Have the time to share your thoughts and opinions wit experts for each topic.

  • Workshop

    Try creating your own work using open sourse license rather than just watching at it.

  • CC Ignite

    Get opportunities to network with CC affiliates around the world, also after the summit.
